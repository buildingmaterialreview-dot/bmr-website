What Affects Cost?

  • Opening Size
    Larger openings require bigger, more expensive lintels and more labour
  • Load-Bearing
    Structural walls require engineer sign-off and temporary supports
  • Access
    Difficult access or upper floors increase labour time and cost
  • Material Choice
    Steel is standard, but concrete or Catnic lintels cost more
  • Finishing
    Making good (plastering, painting) adds to total project cost
